# FXNews Wiki

FXNews is a chart-only MetaTrader 5 Expert Advisor that scans a basket of FX symbols and displays fresh breakout signals on the chart. It is designed for manual traders who want fast awareness across many pairs without opening 50 charts.

## Core Guarantees

- No auto-trading.
- No MT5 economic calendar.
- No external news feed.
- No `WebRequest`.
- No DLLs.
- Chart messages only.
