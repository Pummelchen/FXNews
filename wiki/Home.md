# FXNews Wiki

FXNews is a chart-only MetaTrader 5 custom indicator that scans a basket of FX symbols/timeframes and displays fresh breakout signals on the chart. It is designed for manual traders who want fast awareness across many pairs without opening 50 charts.

## Core Guarantees

- No auto-trading.
- No order placement, position management, or trade execution logic.
- No file logging, CSV writing, or calibration-file reading.
- No external news feed.
- No `WebRequest`.
- No DLLs.
- Chart messages plus MT5 Journal reports for Validation and Autotune only.
